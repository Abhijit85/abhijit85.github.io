About Me

I’m Abhijit Chakraborty, a Solutions Architect at MongoDB and a Ph.D. researcher in Computer Science at Arizona State University, where I work with Dr. Vivek Gupta at the CoRAL Lab (AI Lab).

My work bridges cloud systems, data intelligence, and machine learning, with research spanning Knowledge Graph Embeddings (KGE), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and Federated AI Systems. I’ve contributed to papers accepted at top venues including NeurIPS, ACL, and EMNLP, and I build scalable, production-grade AI architectures combining MongoDB Atlas with advanced LLM and MLOps pipelines on AWS.

This website serves as my personal hub for research updates, open-source projects, and collaborations in trustworthy, interpretable, and scalable AI.

Research Focus

  • Knowledge Graph Embeddings (KGE): Designing interpretable and expressive relational models for reasoning and representation learning.
  • Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG): Building weighted and federated RAG systems that integrate structured and unstructured knowledge efficiently.
  • Federated & Agentic AI Systems: Developing decentralized, privacy-preserving, and cooperative multi-agent frameworks for trustworthy AI.
  • Applied AI & Cloud Architectures: Leveraging MongoDB Atlas and AWS to deploy scalable, LLM-powered data pipelines and intelligent search systems for real-world enterprise use cases.
